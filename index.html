Our Services

CBC Test

A Complete Blood Count (CBC) test is a common blood test used to assess overall health and detect a wide range of disorders, such as anemia, infection, and leukemia.

Thyroid Profile Test

A thyroid profile test measures the levels of thyroid hormones in the blood, including Thyroxine (T4), Triiodothyronine (T3), and Thyroid-Stimulating Hormone (TSH).

Bedside X-Ray

This type of X-ray uses a portable machine that can be brought directly to the patient's bed, providing a convenient and quick way to obtain diagnostic imaging without needing the patient to leave their bed.
